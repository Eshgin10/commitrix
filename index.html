Frequently Asked Questions

What services do you offer?

+

For now Commitrix only offers web design service including front-end development.

How long does it take to complete a project?

+

Project timelines vary depending on complexity, but most landing pages are delivered within 1 week. I prioritize speed without compromising quality.

Is there any refunds?

+

No, I do not offer refunds. Once the project begins, I allocate time, resources, and expertise to deliver your custom website. To ensure transparency: If you have concerns, I'm happy to discuss them before starting. Commitrix's goal is to make sure you love your new website!

Do You Offer Revisions?

+

Yes, I include free minor revisions like text tweaks or small styling adjustments. However, major changes such as redesigning entire sections, restructuring layouts after approval, or adding new features beyond the original scope aren't coverede.
