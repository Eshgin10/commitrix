Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel my subscription at any time?

+

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time with no long-term commitment.

What kind of work can I request?

+

You can request any of the services listed in our Services section. Commitrix exclusively focused on these areas to ensure the highest quality results.

Are there any refunds?

+

No, we do not offer refunds. However, a 7-day free trial is currently available for both Commitrix packages, so you can try our service and only pay if you're satisfied with the workflow.

What happens if I cancel mid-month?

+

If you cancel in the middle of a billing period, you will retain the right to make requests for the remaining time you've already paid for.

How long does it take to complete a project?

+

Timelines depend on the complexity of the project, but we ensure transparency by providing updates every 48 hours for every project.
